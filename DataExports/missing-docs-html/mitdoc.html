MITDoc.tk (grand vision)

  • *ThingsYouDidntKnowExistedAtMIT.tk
  • ThingsYouDidntKnowExisted.tk
  • MITHomepages.tk / MITProjects.tk -- people’s homepages + live projects!
  • *MinimalistHomepages.tk -- list of life-organizing minimalist homepages/way to make one in 5 min. Digital answer to the Existential question
  • WhatShouldIDo.tk -- the beginning of an app that suggests what you should do
  • IfIWentTo.tk / IfIWereAnMITStudent.tk (proto)-- inspirational database of ideas saying what people would do if they went to MIT or Harvard or the like
  • *HackathonProjects.tk -- collaborative Google doc with really good hackathon project ideas.
  • IdeaOverflow.tk -- database of people’s skills, interests, and emails, and soon to be much more. ~jcole@mit.edu, jinpan@MIT.EDU
  • UndergroundCurriculum.tk [todo]
  • MITProgressBars.tk
  • MITSuggestionBox.tk 
  • MITPeople.tk -- People’s tags
  • *SalonDocs.tk -- Notes from intellectual “salons.” attempting to hold one over CPW -- email jcole@mit.edu if you have an idea as to how to find/invite prefrosh
  • FoodsLists.tk -- Good recipes/lists of what people have actually cooked/ate
  • foodsthatgowelltogether.tk
  • HackMode.tk -- Press button whenever you're coding/building something to help others find you
