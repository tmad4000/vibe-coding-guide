🔥 Director of Risk

RoadFlex is a stealth fintech startup modernizing financial services for the underserved trucking and logistics industries. These industries provide the backbone of the US economy. However, the financial infrastructure on which they run is broken. These are underserved industries where the existing financial and payment tools are difficult to use, outdated and prone to fraud.

We are changing that. RoadFlex believes that the hardworking men and women in this sector deserve better. With RoadFlex, customers can rely on the timely movement of capital for our supply chains to function efficiently. We build financial tools and payment products for truckers and fleet managers. We offer them world-class customer service that work with and around the unique constraints of their daily lives. If you would like to join our mission of helping the people manning the backbone of the US economy, let’s talk!

🙋 Why join RoadFlex?

Risk is a critical function to enable sustainable growth at RoadFlex. The candidate will play a key role in establishing robust risk and underwriting processes internally. We are looking for a Risk Lead who will lead and scale this team while building the processes, tools, and reporting to help us manage our next phase of growth and beyond.

  • You will get the chance to be a founding member at a fast-growth fintech startup. We truly believe that it’s your slope — not where you start but how fast we are growing and iterating.
  • We’re a tight-knit, intense team, where you'll learn a ton and have ownership
  • Work with seasoned second-time founders

🤔 What does the Risk team do?

We tackle a diverse range of risk areas including fraud, credit risk and underwriting, collections, dispute management, and risk analytics.Our goal is to take a data-driven, analytical approach to learn from industry best practices while developing solutions that are unique to our industry and customer base

We are currently a small but mighty team. We are looking for someone who is willing to jump head-first into on-the-ground risk issues, quickly identifying immediate paths to resolution, while designing the solutions and building the team that will scale for the long run. At the same time, this role will have a component of Business Operations so the candidate will have the opportunity to expand beyond the risk department

🔑 Key Responsibilities

  • Defining key risk indicators and metrics, and leading informed strategic and operational decisions to measure and manage RoadFlex's risk profile across operations, credit, fraud, and compliance risk
  • Designing and launching risk management processes as we onboard new customers, launch new products, and enter new markets
  • Building and sustaining a world-class team of risk managers and risk analysts who act as the front-line of defense for managing risk at RoadFlex
  • Collaborating closely with other functions including Sales, Product & Engineering, Operations, and Customer Support & Success to identify risk exposure, develop internal tools for risk management, and communicate decisions to the business and to our customer base
  • Developing relationships with external stakeholders to manage compliance, credit bureau reporting, KYB and fraud vendors, and other risk-related partnerships
  • Business Operations responsibilities for both internal and external processes

🧩 RoadFlex might be a good fit if you have

  • 1+ years of experience in risk management, risk operations, and/or fraud
  • Experience scaling a risk function or team at a high-growth fintech start-up, including hiring and managing a team of risk managers and analysts
  • Experience with small business credit, KYB requirements, business payments, and/or card products
  • Experience with or interest in the trucking and logistics industry
  • Data analysis skills, including experience with SQL
