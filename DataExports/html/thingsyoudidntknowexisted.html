How to make a site like thingsyoudidntknowexistedatmit.tk in 5 min.

1. Copy thingsyoudidntknowexistedatmit.tk by going to File > Make a Copy. Edit it/customize for yourself, including title.

2. Get a free domain (extension .tk is free) for your homepage by going to dot.tk > sign in with Google > Domain Panel > Add Domain. Paste in the URL of the doc. Register for 12 months.

3. Add homepage to the list thingsyoudidntknowexisted.tk
