[a]How does something like this resonate with you? "We exist to democratize personal knowledge and enable people to connect with one another"

[b]Is this the final vision of IdeaFlow? Or is the final vision (throwing something out there) along the lines of creating a shared brain so that we can solve humanity's most complex problems efficiently? 

Right now the way it currently reads is that it's a powerful notebook that can surface my own past history as well as my group's history and complete a sentence or paragraph for me.

[c]Have you done user discovery interviews to quantify this? How much time has been lost in trying to find the right notes at the right time? How has this impacted them? Does it vary by industry (i.e. in the healthcare space, doctors who cannot find notes quickly can lose patients).

[d]This sounds cool, but what does it mean? AI has become such a buzzword. Can we add more color to this?
