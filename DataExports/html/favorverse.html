FavorVerse

List of services you could perform that would help people. We’re building the non-engineering equivalent of the open source movement (The “Volunteerism Movement”?) We envision this as something the majority of the developed world becomes a part of to a lesser or greater degree – it will be like: “if you're not part of this cool community improving the world together, what are you doing with your life?

Contribute to the lists at top! Comment under any issue you’d like to support and email jacob+wcg@ideaflow.io if you need help reaching out.
