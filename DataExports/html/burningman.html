Things you might not think of

  • bike lights
  • Combo bike locks
  • Power bank & cables
  • Solar charging powerbank?

  • Tin cup
  • Spoon

  • Blocking dust from your tent
  • Garbage bag
  • Gorilla Tape
  • And/OR Stick on Velcro?

  • Headlamp
  • Extra batteries

  • Electrolytes
  • Lip Balm (with SPF)
  • Flip Flops for transitioning in and out of tent (otherwise you will get dust everywhere)

  • Small journal/notebook and pen so you can leave people notes. And take notes
  • and/or small whiteboard and marker outside your tent for people to leave notes

  • Colored tape to mark which stuff is yours

General Pack List

  • Sleeping Pad or Air Mattress
  • Earplugs and a Sleep Mask
  • Biodegradable Wet Wipes
  • ?hand sanitizer
  • Dust Mask (Strong – P95 > N95 for dust. P100 better but hard to talk) (also check out RZMasks)

  • Hat
  • Sunscreen
  • Ski Goggles (non tinted for night. Perhaps others tinted for day)
  • Personal MOOP (Trash) bag

  • Portable Food – e.g. soylent, ready to eat indian meals
  • Cash (for emergency or ice purchase purpose)
