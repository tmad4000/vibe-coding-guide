Questions
Questions we would ask a person who has attained enlightenment

- Where does the self end and the other begin?
- What would you say to someone who claims to be enjoying duality for now?
- Can I reincarnate in the past?
- How can we distinguish wise action from unwise action? How would we know when to depart from an orthodox pattern for learning meditation to do something more innovative for a modern context?
- Can Maitreya incarnate as an AI?
- Can you teach an AI to give Pointing Out instruction (if needed, imagine you can also control pacing/tone of voice)?
- What questions should I be asking you?
- Can you remember consciousness pre-enlightenment – describe the change from your pre-enlightened experience of the world to post-enlightenment?
- Can this practice help with healing of the physical body and if so how can we apply it (if this is a wise goal)? How can we remove physical injuries that are an obstacle to practice?
- What can we do to engage and produce wellness / support of the physical body while doing the practices you recommend?
- How can we sublimate sexual energy most effectively into spiritual growth?
- How would you motivate, in two sentences, a Westerner not interested in meditation to meditate (if you think it’s worthwhile to suggest this)?
- What do you think of kriya yoga/energy-guiding-consciousness based approaches to cultivation?
