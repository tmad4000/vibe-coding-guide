## Things to Do When Visiting San Diego
Recommended to young couple:
1) go boogie boarding (rent a wetsuit)
If I’m around, I will take you myself! But even if not, do it!
1.5) go for a hike in Torrey Pines State Park
2) go to old town Mexican Café and get the corn tortillas. They are one of the most unbelievable foods I have ever had in my life. You may have to book reservations weeks in advance
c) What’s the plan/got any geographic constraints? La Jolla and Del Mar are super cute and near UCSD
Encinitas/ Cardiff are more hippie artsy surfery

Normally I would say go to Legoland but I’m not sure what the status is
And also Safari Park
