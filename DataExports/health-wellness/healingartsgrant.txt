Brainstorming for Healing Arts Equal Access Grant
http://healingartsgrant.jacobcole.net/ 
A grant to help people gain equal access to important, life-changing healing arts which are sometimes expensive, and often not covered by insurance. Also to assist in care navigation by curating resources and via live help, and to help people with disabilities get assistance.
Examples of such:
-PRP injections for RSI (repetitive strain injury) and arthritis
-Svaroopa.org Yoga Foundations retreat for chronic SI Joint or low back pain
-Seeing an acupuncturist for a condition well treated by TCM, e.g. ovarian cysts
-Therapeutic massage, rolfing, directional non-force chiropractic
-Psychotherapy: EMDR, TRE, Somatic Therapy, IFS, breathwork, etc. Or NVC training.
-Buying Yoga Props or Healing Devices (e.g. cupping therapy cups)
-Going to yoga or pilates class when you're broke
