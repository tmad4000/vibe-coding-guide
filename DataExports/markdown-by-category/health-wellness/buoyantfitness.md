Buoyant Fitness #draft #connectordoc
Exercise system based on using bands paired with unstable surfaces, with the goal of inducing a sense of wakefulness and springiness in the muscles, “expanded proprioception” in the mind, and buoyancy in the body and spirit.

#draft Exercise system based on using bands paired with unstable surfaces, with the goal of inducing a sense of wakefulness and springiness in the muscles, a feeling of “expanded proprioception” (proprioception that extends into the springs that hold you and beyond), and a sense of buoyancy everywhere, even in the mind and spirit.
