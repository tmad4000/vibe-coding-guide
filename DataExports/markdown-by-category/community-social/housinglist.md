We're a group of mid-20s to mid-30s adults who work in a range of professions, from data engineering to founders to design to marketing. Our hobbies are even wider-ranging - we have jazz aficionados, survival enthusiasts, science illustration nerds, wrestling queens, bread bakers, runners, bikers, plant lovers, skilled cooks, roller skaters, and many more. From deep conversations to cuddling around the fire, we generally try to make space to connect and really get to know each other.
How we spend our time
•
🏃‍♀️ Staying Active: Some of us go run together multiple times per week, do fitness videos together, or yoga in the garden. And we wrestle!
•
🏕 Outdoors: Camping (& survival!) trips, hikes, candle-light cuddle puddles in the park, and lots of ocean & beach time
•
👩‍🍳 Cooking. Most evenings you'll see housemates in the kitchen cooking together, and it's a major bonding and decompression time for the house.
•
🎭 Creativity and elaborate weekend productions. Just last weekend we put on an immersive theater birthday party for a housemate and it was an absolute blast.
•
🎵 Music! We do uke & guitar jam sessions (you gotta learn Riptide), play the piano, and dance in the living room
•
🧘‍♀️ Mindfulness. We have a few regular meditators in the house and we generally try to stay present and embrace NVC!
•
🎉 Party (: Most birthdays and major events lead to big fun parties, usually themed. We're looking forward to inviting some other group houses and the larger community to some backyard events when we're post-covid as well.
•
🧊 Chill. Many evenings we just hang out and read together, too :).
Who we're looking for
We're looking to live with supportive, invested, and respectful community members.  We don't care about what you do or how you do it, but we are looking for someone who actively contributes to the community in their own unique way, keeps shared spaces neat, practices good communication, and is as stoked to hang out with us as we are with you! We love makers, creativity, easy-going-ness, and a willingness to do. If you're enthusiastic, kind, and love to be around humans who are the same, this is the place for you! (:
